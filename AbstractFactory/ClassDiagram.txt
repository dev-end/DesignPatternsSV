===============================================================================
                              PATTERN BENEFITS
===============================================================================

✅ FAMILIES: Creates families of related/compatible products
✅ EXTENSIBILITY: Easy to add new product families (HP, Lenovo, etc.)
✅ CONSISTENCY: Products from same family work together
✅ ISOLATION: Client code isolated from concrete product classes
✅ SINGLE RESPONSIBILITY: Each factory creates one product family

===============================================================================
                         ABSTRACT FACTORY vs FACTORY METHOD
===============================================================================

ABSTRACT FACTORY:                    FACTORY METHOD:
• Creates FAMILIES of objects        • Creates SINGLE objects
• Multiple factory methods           • Single factory method
• Product families are related       • Products are independent
• More complex structure             • Simpler structure
• Example: Dell family vs Apple      • Example: Shape factory
